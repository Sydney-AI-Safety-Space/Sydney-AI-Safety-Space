Sydney AI Safety Space

The Sydney AI Safety Space is a volunteer run coworking space based out of the Sydney Knowledge Hub at the University of Sydney. We provide free office space for people working on AI Safety, paid for by donations.

Get Involved

Visit as a Guest

Come cowork with us for free! We welcome anyone interested in AI safety to drop by -- come in for a lunch, or for a day of coworking as a guest. No commitment required -- just reach out via slack or email to let us know you're coming!

Become a Member

Coming in regularly? Become a member! Membership involves:

  • Getting an access card for 24/7 entry
  • Priority access to desks
  • Committing to volunteer for the space (help with events, maintenance, etc.)
  • Actively working on AI safety related projects (e.g. research, policy, study, etc.)

Hot Desking

Our space operates on a hot desking model, which means:

  • No permanently assigned desks - choose any available workspace when you arrive
  • Variety of work environments including standing desks, regular desks, and collaborative areas
  • Lockers available for storing personal items
  • Clean desk policy - clear your workspace at the end of each day
  • First-come, first-served basis for preferred spots

This flexible arrangement promotes collaboration and allows us to accommodate more members in our space.
